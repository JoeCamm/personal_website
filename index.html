About Me

Hey, I'm Joe. I spent my last year traveling across Asia while working remotely as a machine learning engineer. It was the best year of my life, one where I was actually living, not just existing in a routine. I'm looking for remote work where I can keep doing that: building things that matter while seeing the world.

For the last three years I've worked in computer vision, mostly crack detection for infrastructure inspection. It sounds boring, but training models gives me this sense of commitment to them. I want them to succeed and do well, and I feel oddly responsible to help them. I'm not sure this is what I'll do forever, but I know these skills let me make a difference I couldn't make otherwise, and I want to keep learning.

It's scary putting your work and ideas out in public, even when placing them in some obscure corner of the internet. But I wanted a space to share who I am, not just what I'm qualified for. If you would like to get in touch, feel free to contact me here.
